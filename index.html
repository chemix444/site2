About the Mall

More than
just shopping

Mall of India is Naperville's premier South Asian lifestyle destination — a space where shopping, dining, family and community come together in one vibrant, welcoming hub.

50+ Stores & Services
Fashion & Apparel

From traditional sarees and lehengas to contemporary South Asian fusion wear — curated boutiques for every occasion.

Authentic Dining

A food court bursting with regional Indian cuisine — from street chaat to royal thalis, delivering real flavors from home.

Events & Culture

Festivals, performances, cultural celebrations and community gatherings held year-round in our event spaces.

Kids & Family

Dedicated children's activity zones, educational programs, and family-focused services within the mall.

Community Services

Health, education, financial and immigration services serving the greater South Asian community of Chicagoland.

Advertising & Leasing

Premium retail spaces and targeted advertising opportunities reaching the entire Chicagoland South Asian market.

The Experience

Rooted in
South Asian culture

For the South Asian community of Chicagoland, Mall of India is more than a destination — it's a gathering place. A space where traditions are honored, families reconnect, and new memories are made every day.

Diverse Retail Ecosystem
From groceries and spices to high-end jewelry and electronics, our vendors bring the full spectrum of South Asian retail to one address.
Year-Round Celebrations
Diwali, Navratri, Eid, Holi — we celebrate all with elaborate programs, performances and community gatherings.
Family-First Design
Thoughtfully designed spaces for children, elderly visitors and families of all sizes — comfort is never an afterthought.
